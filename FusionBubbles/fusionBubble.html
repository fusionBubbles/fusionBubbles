About Us

Fusion Bubbles is born from the union of two simple yet creative meanings. On one hand, it represents the fusion of our names – Federica, Jennifer, Denis, and Alessio – four friends sharing a common passion for good food and fresh ideas. On the other hand, it celebrates the true essence of our shop: a fusion of flavors, cultures, and creativity in every bubble tea we prepare. It’s a name that tells our story, our friendship, and our desire to offer a special experience to anyone who wants to try it.

Our shop offers a loyalty program with which our customers can gain points which grant them access to exclusive offers.
